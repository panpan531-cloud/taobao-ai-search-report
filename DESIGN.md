# Taobao AI Search Report Visual Brief

## Style Prompt

Build a premium product strategy research site for Taobao search and AI shopping assistance. The experience should feel like a disciplined consulting report brought to life: quiet, data-rich, confident, and product-aware. Use a warm paper canvas, charcoal text, restrained Taobao orange, and deep teal for analytical contrast. Motion should reveal structure rather than decorate: scroll-triggered section builds, line traces, proportion fills, staged interface mocks, and subtle background field movement.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#F7F2E9` warm paper
- Canvas deep: `#EFE4D4` section contrast
- Ink: `#1B1D1A` primary text
- Muted ink: `#686158` secondary text
- Taobao orange: `#FF6A00` brand/action signal
- Deep teal: `#0C5B56` analytical contrast
- Olive: `#6F7E35` positive/quality signal
- Risk red: `#B5442F` negative friction signal
- Line: `#D8CCBA` hairline dividers

## Typography

- Display / section statements: `Fraunces`, Georgia, serif
- Body / interface: `IBM Plex Sans`, Arial, sans-serif
- Data / labels: `IBM Plex Mono`, monospace

## Motion Rules

- Keep section titles restrained; hierarchy comes from spacing, contrast, and data modules.
- Use scroll-triggered reveals with staggered entrances and chart fills.
- Prefer editorial transitions: clipped panels, horizontal rules, SVG paths, and subtle parallax.
- Interactions should feel precise: 160-260ms hover/focus, measured easing, no bouncy consumer UI.
- Ambient motion should be low-opacity and slow enough to feel like research instrumentation.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not mention interviews or hiring anywhere in visible UI text.
- Do not make a generic hero landing page or oversized marketing headline.
- Do not use one-note orange/beige styling; orange is an accent, not the whole palette.
- Do not imply Xiaohongshu sampling is exhaustive; clearly mark source constraints.
- Do not use huge dashboard cards or tiny report text; the page must be readable in discussion.
